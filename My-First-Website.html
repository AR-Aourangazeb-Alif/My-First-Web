I had always wanted to visit St. Martin Island, the only coral island in Bangladesh. It was a dream of mine to see the turquoise water, the white sand, and the coconut trees. So when I got the chance to join a group of friends for a three-day trip, I was overjoyed.

We took a bus from Dhaka to Teknaf, the southernmost point of the mainland. From there, we boarded a ship that would take us to the island.
