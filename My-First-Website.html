My Travel Blog

I had always dreamed of visiting St. Martin Island, the only coral island in Bangladesh. I had heard so much about its natural beauty, its crystal clear water, its rich marine life, and its friendly people. I finally got the chance to go there last year, and it was one of the best trips of my life.

I took a bus from Dhaka to Teknaf, the southernmost point of the mainland. From there, I boarded a ship that would take me to the island. The journey was about three hours long, but I didn't mind. I enjoyed the view of the Bay of Bengal, the fresh breeze, and the occasional sight of dolphins and . . .
