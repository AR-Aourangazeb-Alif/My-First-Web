I had always wanted to visit St. Martin's Island, the only coral island in Bangladesh. I had heard so much about its natural beauty, its turquoise water, its white sand beaches, and its rich marine life. So, when I got the chance to join a group of friends for a three-day trip, I was overjoyed.

We took a bus from Dhaka to Teknaf, the southernmost point of mainland Bangladesh. From there, we boarded a ship that would take us to the island. The journey was about two hours long, but it felt like an eternity. I was eager to see the island with my own eyes.
